Plans for studying in Spain

Universidad de Alcalá

The University of Alcala is a public university located in Alcala de Henares, Madrid, Spain, founded on 1 July 1977. It has about 26,000 students and 1,750 professors. Its abbreviated name is UAH.

Food

Spanish cuisine, represented by paella, tortilla and gazpacho, is probably loved by many Japanese people. Spain is also the world's largest producer of olive oil and it is used in almost all dishes.

World Heritage Sites

Spain is a culturally diverse country, and World Heritage Sites are everywhere. 　　　　　　　　　　Spain is famous for its unique World Heritage Sites, including the architectural masterpieces of the genius Gaudi , the ruins of the Roman Empire, the Islamic architecture of Cordoba and Granada, and the ancient city of Toledo.
